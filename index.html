Powerful Features, Trusted by Institutions

Classes – Everything in One Place

Access attendance, chats, attachments, assignments, and syllabus for every subject in a single, organized view.

Calendar – Stay Updated, Always

All university events, holidays, and academic schedules are seamlessly integrated, ensuring no missed deadlines.

Information Portal – Instant Access to Key Updates

Get real-time updates on university events, holidays, and academic calendars—all in one central hub.

Tools – Essential Campus Utilities

From Resume/CV Maker and Location Finder to Public Transport, Emergency Help, and even a Dustbin Locator—all the tools students need, in one place.

Alumni & Community Building – Stronger Connections, Brighter Opportunities

Stay connected with alumni for networking, mentorship, funding, and career opportunities.

Timetable Automation – Hassle-Free Scheduling

Automatically managed via the admin panel, ensuring error-free, optimized timetables.

Attendance & Result Tracking – Real-Time Performance Insights

Students can check subject-wise attendance and semester-wise results at a glance.

Complaint Resolution System – AI-Powered Grievance Management

A structured, efficient feedback and complaint resolution system that ensures concerns are addressed transparently and swiftly.
